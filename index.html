About

Montecito Racing was founded in the USA, built to expand racing opportunities for all skill levels. From our drivers to our staff, we strive to instill teamwork, comradery, and respect everyday. We keep the standards simple: clear goals, shared data, and honest reviews after every session. We prepare, execute, and own the result. Clean stints, smart traffic management, and repeatable pace come first. Over time, that work builds a record. Our goal is a tradition of racing dominance earned by lap times and finishes.
